About Me

A computer vision developer, AI researcher, and a fourth-year Mechatronics Engineering student. As the founder of EJAZTECH.AI, I specialize in developing AI-driven solutions that address local challenges in Nigeria and Africa. I have collaborated with various companies to build tailored AI models for their specific needs. Additionally, I lead a mentorship program through my company, guiding aspiring youths in their AI careers. My mission is to empower individuals to innovate and create transformative technologies for the betterment of Nigeria and the broader African continent.
